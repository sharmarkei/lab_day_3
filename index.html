About Me

Hello, my name is Sharmarke Ibrahim. I was born April 29, 1994, which makes me 23 years old. I'm the second oldest of 6 siblings (yes I know that's a lot). Before starting Code Fellows, I was a Junior at the University of Washington Tacoma. I was majoring in Economics but shortly after starting I noticed that it was something I didn't have a passion for.

After a few quarter into my major, I meant a few friends who were doing Computer Science. I would see them always typing their code and talking about making the website/application do mind blowing things. I wanted to switch to a CS major but I had already invest 4+ years of my life on working towards my Econ degree. I just didn't have the time to start over and do all the pre-reqs required for the CS major. That was when I decided to go to Code Fellows. I knew what they taught here was increcible based on what my brother, an alumni of the program, told me and decided I would make the switch to web development. Quiet possibly the best decision I've made.
